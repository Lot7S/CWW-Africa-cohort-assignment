Maduechesi Chibuike

Founder, story creator and artist of Legacy Comics.
Self taught digital artist

I am a self taught artist and storyline creator. Since I first held my my very first x-men comics in my hand, it has been my dream to replicate something unique but similar. Unique in the sense that it puts african heroes and villians, who have lived our lifestyles, on the map. But similar in the sense that we are recreating our own universe of wonderful tales and beings. I have worked in 2D NFTprojects like Deadpool Adventures and Black Toggle . Each, project came with its unique cocepts and characters. So, I wouldn't be mistaken if I called this my passion.
